Play Bingo

To play my bingo game, assign numbers to your board and to play, click the roll dice and click the checkboxes until you get a row, column or diagonal, 7 is most common in rolls and numbers further from 7 are rarer (to insert numbers, you can click dropboxes, click randomize button for a random board, and rolling dice gives you a code you can insert into the form to save a board)
